|-------------|----------------|--------|

## Implementation Notes

### Data Requirements
- Clean, deduplicated contact data
- Complete company information
- Tracked website activity
- Email engagement metrics
- Event participation records
- Sales outcome data for validation

### Success Metrics
- MQL to SQL conversion rate by score range
- Sales cycle length correlation with score
- Revenue attribution to scored leads
- Sales team adoption and satisfaction
- Model accuracy (precision/recall)

### Maintenance Schedule
- Weekly: Score distribution analysis
- Monthly: Threshold optimization
- Quarterly: Point allocation review
- Annually: Complete model overhaul

### Integration Points
- CRM lead/contact records
- Marketing automation platforms
- Website analytics
- Email marketing tools
- Event management systems
- Intent data providers
