# Food Overview — Barcelona

Barcelona is a food paradise. Unlike Madrid (hearty Castilian), Barcelona offers Mediterranean cuisine — seafood, rice dishes, and Catalan specialties. The coast location means excellent fish. Add world-class modernist restaurants and diverse international options.

## What Barcelona Does Well

| Cuisine | Why | Best Areas |
|---------|-----|------------|
| **Catalan** | Local tradition: pa amb tomàquet, escalivada, calcots | Gràcia, Born, Barceloneta |
| **Seafood** | Fresh from Mediterranean | Barceloneta, Born, Poblenou |
| **Rice dishes** | Paella, arròs negre, fideuà | Barceloneta (careful of traps), Poblenou |
| **Basque pintxos** | Strong Basque community | Born, Gràcia |
| **Tapas** | Spanish tradition, well-executed | Poble Sec, Born |
| **Modernist/fine dining** | World-class (Disfrutar, Tickets) | Eixample, various |
| **Vermouth** | Strong tradition | Gràcia, Sant Antoni, Born |
| **Brunch** | International influence | Born, Gràcia, Poblenou |

**Key insight:** Barcelona's food is Mediterranean with Catalan identity. Less meat-heavy than Madrid, more seafood and vegetables.

## The Menú del Día

**What:** 3-course lunch (primer + segon + postres) + bread + drink
**Price:** €12-18 typically
**When:** Weekday lunch, usually 1pm-4pm
**Where:** Most non-tourist restaurants

**Example:**
- Primer plat: Amanida or escudella
- Segon plat: Pollastre or bacallà
- Postres: Crema catalana or fruit
- Beguda: Wine, beer, or water
- **Total: €14**

**Tips:**
- Best value meal anywhere
- Quality varies — ask locals
- Tourist areas often don't offer it
- Best ones: chalkboard outside, locals inside

## Timing (Important!)

| Meal | Time | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| **Esmorzar** | 8-10am | Coffee + pastry |
| **Second breakfast** | 11am | Entrepà or more substantial |
| **Dinar** | 1:30-3:30pm | MAIN meal, everything closes |
| **Berenar** | 5-7pm | Coffee/snack |
| **Sopar** | 9-10:30pm | Lighter than lunch |
| **Late night** | After midnight | Tapas, pintxos |

**Warning:** 
- Restaurants often don't open for dinner until 8:30-9pm
- Eating at 7pm = eating with tourists
- Lunch at 12:30pm = empty restaurant

## Tipping Culture

| Situation | Tip |
|-----------|-----|
| **Casual tapas/bar** | Round up or nothing |
| **Restaurant** | 5-10% if good service |
| **Fine dining** | 10% (check if service included) |
| **Coffee** | Round up |

**Note:** Tipping is appreciated but not expected. Service is included in prices.
