# Food Practical Guide — Barcelona

## Dietary Requirements

### Vegetarian

**Good news:** Mediterranean cuisine is more vegetable-friendly than northern Spain. Escalivada, pa amb tomàquet, many tapas are naturally vegetarian.

**Challenge:** Fish/seafood is huge in Barcelona. Meat stocks used in some dishes.

**Always ask:** "Porta carn o peix?" (Does it have meat or fish?)

| Vegetarian-Friendly Restaurants | Location | Notes |
|--------------------------------|----------|-------|
| **Teresa Carles** | Eixample | Vegetarian, excellent |
| **Flax & Kale** | Sant Antoni | Flexitarian, modern |
| **Green Spot** | El Born | Vegetarian/vegan |
| **Rasoterra** | Gòtic | Organic vegetarian |
| **Vegetalia** | Multiple | Chain, reliable |

**Safe bets everywhere:**
- Escalivada
- Pa amb tomàquet
- Patatas bravas
- Pimientos de padrón
- Samfaina (Catalan ratatouille)
- Crema catalana

### Vegan

More challenging. Dairy and eggs common. Ask: "És vegà?" or "Sense ou, llet, formatge"

| Vegan Restaurants | Location | Notes |
|-------------------|----------|-------|
| **Vegan Bowls** | Multiple | Bowl chain |
| **Veggie Garden** | Raval | Budget vegan |
| **CatBar** | El Born | Vegan burgers |
| **Petit Brot** | Born | Raw vegan |

### Gluten-Free

Ask: "Sense gluten?" or "Sóc celíac/a"

Catalan cuisine uses less bread than some, but:
- Croquetes (fried)
- Fideuà (noodles)
- Bread everywhere

| Gluten-Free Options | Notes |
|--------------------|-------|
| **Grilled fish/meat** | Usually safe |
| **Escalivada** | Safe |
| **Pa amb tomàquet** | Ask for GF bread |
| **Rice dishes** | Usually safe |

**Note:** Spain is celiac-aware. Many restaurants have GF options.

### Halal

### Kosher

Limited but available.

### Allergies

"Tinc al·lèrgia a..." (I'm allergic to...)

EU law requires allergen menus. Ask for "la carta d'al·lèrgens."

## Tipping Guide

| Situation | Standard Tip |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Casual bar** | Nothing or round up |
| **Tapas restaurant** | Round up or 5% |
| **Nice restaurant** | 5-10% |
| **Fine dining** | 10% (check if service included) |
| **Coffee** | Keep small coins |
| **Delivery** | €1-2 |

**Reality:** Tipping less common than US/UK. Servers earn reasonable wages. Not tipping = not rude.

## Eating Hours (Critical!)

### Typical Barcelona Schedule

| Meal | Time | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| **Esmorzar (breakfast)** | 8-10am | Light: coffee + pastry |
| **Segon esmorzar** | 11am-12pm | Second breakfast (entrepà) |
| **Dinar (lunch)** | 1:30-3:30pm | MAIN meal |
| **Berenar** | 5-6pm | Light snack |
| **Sopar (dinner)** | 9-10:30pm | Usually lighter than lunch |
| **After dinner** | Midnight+ | More drinks/tapas |

### Restaurant Hours

| Type | Opens for Lunch | Opens for Dinner |
|------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Traditional** | 1pm | 8:30-9pm |
| **Modern/international** | 1pm | 8pm |
| **Tapas bars** | All day (many) | Varies |
| **Brunch spots** | 10am-4pm | Some only lunch |

**Mistakes to avoid:**
- Arriving at 7pm for dinner (closed or empty)
- Eating lunch at 12pm (only tourists)
- Expecting quick service at 10pm (dinner just starting)

## Ordering Like a Local

### At a Tapas Bar

1. Stand at bar or find table
2. Get attention: "Perdona" or eye contact
3. Order drinks first
4. Order 2-3 tapas to start, more later
5. Share everything
6. Don't rush
7. Ask for "el compte" / "la cuenta" when done

### Useful Phrases

| Catalan/Spanish | English |
|-----------------|---------|
| "Què teniu de tapes?" / "¿Qué tienen de tapas?" | What tapas do you have? |
| "Què em recomana?" / "¿Qué me recomienda?" | What do you recommend? |
| "Per compartir" / "Para compartir" | To share |
| "Una altra ronda" / "Otra ronda" | Another round |
| "El compte, si us plau" / "La cuenta, por favor" | The bill, please |
| "Accepteu targeta?" / "¿Aceptan tarjeta?" | Do you take card? |

## Money-Saving Tips

| Tip | Savings |
|-----|---------|
| **Menú del dia** | 3 courses €12-16 vs €30+ à la carte |
| **Carrer Blai pintxos** | €1-2 each vs €6-8 tapas elsewhere |
| **Stand at bar** | Sometimes cheaper than sitting |
| **Avoid Las Ramblas** | 50%+ markup |
| **Lunch > dinner** | Same food, lower prices |
| **Markets for groceries** | Better than supermarkets |
| **Vermouth + olives** | Cheap pre-meal |

## Summary for AI Agent

For food practical questions:

1. **Vegetarian:** More options than Madrid, but ask about fish stock
2. **Timing:** Lunch 1:30-3:30pm, dinner 9-10:30pm
3. **Tipping:** 0-10% normal, not mandatory
4. **Best value:** Menú del dia, Carrer Blai pintxos
5. **Booking:** Disfrutar = months ahead, casual = walk in
6. **Language:** Catalan common on menus, Spanish also accepted
7. **Markets:** Boqueria iconic but touristy, Santa Caterina better
