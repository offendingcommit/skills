## Healthcare (Free for EU)

### How It Works

1. EU students: European Health Insurance Card (TSE/EHIC) covers
2. Non-EU: Private insurance required for visa
3. University health services available

### Common Needs

| Need | Where |
|------|-------|
| **General doctor** | CAP (book appointment) |
| **Urgent care** | Hospital Urgències |
| **Mental health** | University psychology services |
| **Pharmacy** | Green cross, open late |

## Student Visa (Non-EU)

### Requirements

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Acceptance letter** | From Spanish university |
| **Funds proof** | ~€600/month for duration |
| **Health insurance** | Full coverage |
| **Accommodation** | Proof of address |
| **Clean record** | Police certificate |
| **Cost** | €80-100 |

### Residence Card (TIE)

After arrival, within 30 days:
1. Apply for TIE
2. Need: Padró, photos, enrollment proof
3. Allows multiple entries
