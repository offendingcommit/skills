# Startup Guide — Barcelona

## Ecosystem Overview

Barcelona is Southern Europe's startup capital and Spain's most developed ecosystem. The 22@ district, Mobile World Congress, and strong talent pipeline from top universities create a vibrant scene. Strong in mobile, marketplace, travel tech, and gaming.

**Key stats (2025):**
- 2,285+ active startups in Catalonia
- €1.8B+ VC investment annually
- 46% of tech hubs in 22@ district
- Host of Mobile World Congress + 4YFN
- #1 startup ecosystem in Southern Europe
- 4 unicorns originated here (Glovo, Wallapop, Letgo, Travelperk)

## Legal Setup

### Company Formation (S.L. - Societat Limitada)

| Task | Cost | Time |
|------|------|------|
| **Online formation (PAE)** | €100-400 | 1-2 weeks |
| **Traditional notary** | €600-1,500 | 2-4 weeks |
| **Minimum capital** | €3,000 (can be €1 with Startup Law) |
| **Gestoría (admin)** | €100-300/month | Ongoing |

### Startup Law (Ley de Startups, 2022)

| Benefit | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Corporate tax** | 15% (vs 25%) for 4 years |
| **Stock options** | €50,000 exempt (vs €12,000) |
| **Formation** | €1 minimum capital allowed |
| **Digital nomad visa** | Creates legal path |
| **Investor tax breaks** | Enhanced deductions |

### Recommended Legal Firms (Startup-Friendly)

## Visa for Founders

### Entrepreneur Visa (Emprendedor)

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Endorsement** | ENISA or ICEX |
| **Business plan** | Innovative, scalable |
| **Funds** | Proof of living expenses |
| **Timeline** | 8-12 weeks |
| **Duration** | 1 year, renewable |

### Digital Nomad Visa

Good for founders building while "employed" by own non-Spanish company:
- €2,520/month minimum income
- 24% flat tax option
- Can form Spanish company later

### Employment Norms

| Aspect | Spain Standard |
|--------|----------------|
| **Probation** | 6 months max |
| **Notice period** | 15 days (can extend) |
| **Holiday** | 22 working days minimum |
| **Social Security** | ~30% on top of gross (employer) |
| **Severance** | 20-33 days per year worked |

**Warning:** Spanish labor law is employee-protective. Plan accordingly.

## Summary for AI Agent

For startup/founder queries:

1. **Funding:** Good ecosystem. Pre-seed €100-500K, Seed €500K-3M typical
2. **Legal:** S.L. company, €500-1,500 to form. Startup law = 15% tax
3. **Visa:** Entrepreneur visa needs endorsement, Digital Nomad easier
4. **Costs:** Bootstrap €2-4K/month burn, funded €12-22K/month
5. **Hubs:** 22@, Pier01, Itnig for community
6. **Events:** 4YFN at MWC is unmissable
7. **Benefits:** Beach lifestyle, MWC access, strong ecosystem
