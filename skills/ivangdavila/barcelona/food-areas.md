# Best Eating Areas — Barcelona

## El Born

**The best neighborhood for food.** Historic streets, excellent restaurants from tapas to fine dining.

| Vibe | Trendy, historic, foodie |
|------|-------------------------|
| Best for | Tapas, seafood, cocktails |
| Price | €€-€€€ |
| Best time | Evening, weekend brunch |

### Must-Visit

| Place | Specialty | Price |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Cal Pep | Seafood tapas (chef decides) | €€€ |
| El Xampanyet | Cava + anchovies (traditional) | € |
| Bar del Pla | Modern tapas | €€ |
| La Vinya del Senyor | Wine bar, cathedral view | €€ |
| Paradiso | Speakeasy cocktails (world's best bar) | €€€ |
| Picnic | Modern Catalan | €€ |

**Crawl route:** El Xampanyet → Bar del Pla → La Vinya del Senyor → Paradiso

## Poble Sec

**Foodie heaven.** Carrer Blai is Barcelona's best tapas street.

| Vibe | Local, foodie, value |
|------|---------------------|
| Best for | Pintxos crawl, tapas |
| Price | € |
| Best time | Evening |

### Carrer Blai (The Street)

| Place | Specialty | Price |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| **Blai 9** | Pintxos | € |
| **La Tasqueta de Blai** | Pintxos | € |
| **Koska Taverna** | Basque pintxos | € |
| **Quimet i Quimet** (nearby) | Conservas, montaditos | €€ |

**How it works:** Walk in, grab pintxos (€1-2 each), get drink, pay by toothpicks.

### Beyond Blai

| Place | Specialty | Price |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Tickets | Adrià brothers, modernist | €€€ |
| Bar Seco | Vermouth | € |
| El Sortidor | Traditional bar | € |

## Barceloneta

**Seafood neighborhood.** Beach + fish. But beware tourist traps.

| Vibe | Beach, seafood, touristy |
|------|-------------------------|
| Best for | Paella, seafood, beach lunch |
| Price | €€-€€€ |
| Best time | Lunch |

### Worth It

| Place | Specialty | Price |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| La Cova Fumada | Bomba (they invented it) | € |
| Can Paixano | Cava + jamón, standing | € |
| Can Ros | Rice dishes | €€ |
| La Mar Salada | Seafood | €€ |
| Can Solé | Traditional seafood | €€€ |

### AVOID

| Place | Why |
|-------|-----|
| **Beachfront first line (Passeig Marítim)** | Tourist trap paella, €20+ for mediocre |
| **Anywhere with photos of paella** | Bad sign |
| **Restaurant touts** | If they need to grab you, food isn't good |

**Tip:** Walk inland 2-3 blocks from beach for better quality.
