# Local Services Guide — Barcelona

## Healthcare

### Public Healthcare (CatSalut)

**Access:** Through Catalan Health Service (CatSalut) via Social Security enrollment.

| Service | How to Access |
|---------|---------------|
| **GP (metge de capçalera)** | Assigned CAP (Centro de Atención Primaria) |
| **Specialist** | Referral from GP |
| **Emergency (Urgències)** | Any hospital, 24/7 |
| **Mental health** | Referral from GP |

**Booking appointments:**
- App: La Meva Salut
- Phone: 061
- Online: catsalut.gencat.cat

### Hospitals

| Hospital | Type | Location | Notes |
|----------|------|----------|-------|
| **Hospital Clínic** | Public, major | Eixample | Excellent, teaching |
| **Hospital de Sant Pau** | Public, historic | Guinardó | UNESCO heritage |
| **Hospital del Mar** | Public | Barceloneta | Near beach |
| **Hospital Vall d'Hebron** | Public, largest | Horta | Biggest in Catalonia |
| **Clínica Teknon** | Private | Sarrià | Premium |
| **Hospital Quirón** | Private | Multiple | Private chain |

### Pharmacies (Farmàcies)

- **Green cross** = pharmacy
- **24-hour pharmacies:** At least one per district
- **Find nearest:** farmaguia.net or pharmacy apps
- **What pharmacists can do:** Recommend meds for minor issues

### Dental

Public dental limited to extractions and emergencies. Most people use private.

| Clinic Type | Cost (Checkup) |
|-------------|----------------|
| **Private chain** | €30-50 |
| **Private dentist** | €40-80 |

### Mental Health

| Resource | Access |
|----------|--------|
| **Public psychologist** | GP referral (long waits) |
| **Private therapy** | €50-100/session |
| **Telèfon de l'Esperança** | 93 414 48 48 |
| **Crisis line** | 024 |

## Pets

### Veterinarians

- Many neighborhood vets
- Emergency 24h: Hospital Veterinari del Maresme, others
- Cost: €35-50 routine visit

### Pet Registration

**Required:**
- Microchip
- Register at Ajuntament
- Census fee: ~€20-40

### Pet-Friendly Places

- **Beaches:** Some dog-friendly (check rules)
- **Parks:** Most allow dogs
- **Restaurants:** Many terraces allow
