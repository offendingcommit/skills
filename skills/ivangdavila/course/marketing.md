# Course Marketing & Sales

## Sales Page Structure

**Essential sections (in order):**
1. **Headline** — Transformation promise (From X to Y)
2. **Problem** — What pain they're experiencing
3. **Solution** — Your course solves this
4. **Curriculum** — What they'll learn (modules)
5. **Instructor** — Why you're credible
6. **Testimonials** — Social proof
7. **Pricing** — Options and value justification
8. **FAQ** — Objection handling
9. **Guarantee** — Risk reversal
10. **Final CTA** — Clear call to action

**Headline formulas:**
- "How to [Achieve Result] in [Timeframe] Without [Pain Point]"
- "The [Number]-Step System to [Desired Outcome]"
- "From [Before State] to [After State]: A Complete [Topic] Course"

**Pricing presentation:**
- Show total value ("$X value")
- Cross out higher price
- Reveal actual price
- Break down to daily/weekly cost
- Payment plan option if high-ticket


## Email Launch Sequence

### Pre-launch (7-14 days before)
| Day | Email | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| -14 | Announcement | Course is coming, join waitlist |
| -10 | Story/Problem | Share why you created this |
| -7 | Content preview | Free value, builds anticipation |
| -3 | Testimonials | Social proof from beta/early users |
| -1 | Tomorrow! | Final reminder, urgency |

| Day | Email | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| 0 | Cart open | Full details, link to buy |

## Promotional Content

**Content types to generate:**
- Social posts announcing course
- Short video clips (teasers, testimonials)
- Carousel posts explaining what's included
- Stories/reels with behind-the-scenes
- Long-form content (blog/video) as lead magnets

**Platform adaptation:**
| Platform | Format | Length | Focus |
|----------|--------|--------|-------|
| Instagram | Carousel, Reel | Short | Visual, emotional |
| LinkedIn | Text post, article | Medium | Professional, results |
| Twitter/X | Thread | Short | Quick tips, hooks |
| YouTube | Video | Long | Deep dive, SEO |
| TikTok | Short video | Very short | Hook, personality |


## Objection Handling (FAQ)

**Common objections to address:**
1. "Is this right for my level?" → Explain who it's for and not for
2. "How long will it take?" → Realistic timeline with flexibility
3. "What if I don't have time?" → Lifetime access, self-paced
4. "What makes this different?" → Your unique approach/method
5. "Can I get support?" → Community, Q&A, office hours
6. "What's the guarantee?" → Refund policy clearly stated
7. "Why this price?" → Value breakdown, ROI calculation


## Pricing Strategy

**Factors to consider:**
- Competitor pricing (where do you position?)
- Transformation value (what's the outcome worth?)
- Your credibility/audience size
- Content depth (mini-course vs comprehensive)
- Support level (self-paced vs cohort vs coaching)

**Pricing tiers:**
| Tier | Includes | Price range |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Basic | Core content only | $97-297 |
| Standard | + Community + templates | $297-497 |
| Premium | + Coaching + certification | $497-2000+ |

**Launch vs evergreen pricing:**
- Launch: Lower price, limited time, urgency
- Evergreen: Regular price, always available
- Hybrid: Periodic launches with urgency, evergreen between
