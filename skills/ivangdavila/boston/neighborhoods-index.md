## Neighborhood Personalities

### The "Classic Boston" Feel
- Beacon Hill: Brick rowhouses, gaslamps, old money
- North End: Italian restaurants, narrow streets, tourists
- Back Bay: Victorian brownstones, shopping, Newbury Street

### Young Professional Energy
- South End: Best restaurants, LGBTQ-friendly, brownstones
- Somerville (Union/Davis): Music venues, craft beer, diverse
- Cambridge (Central Square): Gritty-creative, bars, startups

### Student Central
- Allston/Brighton: BU, BC students, cheap food, house parties
- Fenway: Northeastern, Berklee, Red Sox adjacent
- Cambridge (Harvard Square): Academic tourists, bookstores

### Family-Friendly Suburbs
- Brookline: Top schools, walkable, Green Line access
- Newton: Excellent schools, more suburban
- Arlington/Lexington: Revolutionary history, good schools

### Up-and-Coming
- East Boston: Diverse food scene, Blue Line, waterfront
- Dorchester: Vietnamese district, Ashmont/Fields Corner
- Somerville (East): Assembly Row development
