# Climate & Weather — Rome

## Overview

Rome has a **Mediterranean climate**: hot dry summers, mild wet winters. Spring and fall are ideal for visiting.

### Annual Summary

| Season | Months | Temp Range | Rain | Verdict |
|--------|--------|------------|------|---------|
| Spring | Mar-May | 10-24°C | Moderate | Best time |
| Summer | Jun-Aug | 22-35°C | Low | Hot, crowded |
| Fall | Sep-Nov | 12-25°C | Moderate | Best time |
| Winter | Dec-Feb | 4-14°C | Higher | Cool, fewer tourists |

## Month by Month

### January
- **Temp**: 3-12°C
- **Rain**: 8 days avg
- **Crowds**: Low
- **Best for**: Museums, lower prices
- **Note**: After holidays lull, some closures

### February
- **Temp**: 4-13°C
- **Rain**: 7 days avg
- **Crowds**: Low
- **Best for**: Indoor sights, deals
- **Events**: Carnival (dates vary)

### March
- **Temp**: 6-16°C
- **Rain**: 8 days avg
- **Crowds**: Rising
- **Best for**: Shoulder season
- **Note**: Weather variable, pack layers

### April
- **Temp**: 9-19°C
- **Rain**: 7 days avg
- **Crowds**: High (Easter)
- **Best for**: Outdoor everything
- **Events**: Easter, major crowds
- **Note**: Book ahead for Easter week

### May
- **Temp**: 13-24°C
- **Rain**: 5 days avg
- **Crowds**: High
- **Best for**: Perfect weather
- **Note**: Increasingly busy, outdoor dining perfect

### June
- **Temp**: 17-29°C
- **Rain**: 3 days avg
- **Crowds**: Very high
- **Best for**: Long days, events
- **Events**: Estate Romana begins
- **Note**: Getting hot

### July
- **Temp**: 20-32°C
- **Rain**: 2 days avg
- **Crowds**: High (but some Romans leave)
- **Best for**: If you can handle heat
- **Note**: Very hot, bring water everywhere

### August
- **Temp**: 20-32°C
- **Rain**: 2 days avg
- **Crowds**: Tourist high, Roman low
- **Best for**: Only if you like heat
- **Events**: Ferragosto (15th)
- **Note**: Many Romans on holiday, some closures

### September
- **Temp**: 17-27°C
- **Rain**: 5 days avg
- **Crowds**: Moderate-high
- **Best for**: Excellent balance
- **Note**: Romans return, summer heat fading

### October
- **Temp**: 12-22°C
- **Rain**: 8 days avg
- **Crowds**: Moderate
- **Best for**: Great weather, fewer crowds
- **Note**: Variable, pack rain gear

### November
- **Temp**: 8-16°C
- **Rain**: 10 days avg
- **Crowds**: Low
- **Best for**: Budget travel
- **Note**: Rainier, but manageable

### December
- **Temp**: 4-12°C
- **Rain**: 8 days avg
- **Crowds**: Moderate (holidays)
- **Best for**: Christmas atmosphere
- **Events**: Christmas markets, New Year
- **Note**: Cold but festive

## What to Pack

### Spring (Mar-May)
- Layers (warm morning, warmer afternoon)
- Light jacket or sweater
- Comfortable walking shoes
- Small umbrella
- Sunglasses
- Light scarf (church modesty)

### Summer (Jun-Aug)
- Light, breathable clothing
- Sunscreen (essential)
- Hat for sun
- Comfortable sandals/shoes
- Light cover for churches
- Reusable water bottle
- Small day bag

### Fall (Sep-Nov)
- Similar to spring
- Warmer jacket for late Oct/Nov
- Rain jacket
- Umbrella
- Layers

### Winter (Dec-Feb)
- Warm coat
- Sweaters
- Scarf, gloves
- Waterproof shoes
- Umbrella
- Layers for heated interiors

## Climate Impacts on Activities

### Sightseeing Strategy

**Summer (Hot)**
- Start early (8am) for outdoor sites
- Siesta 1-4pm (like locals)
- Evening activities after 5pm
- Prioritize indoor sites midday
- Stay hydrated

**Winter (Cool/Wet)**
- No early morning rush needed
- More museum/indoor time
- Shorter days (dark by 5pm)
- Churches and galleries
- Warm up in cafés

### Specific Site Tips

**Colosseum/Forum**
- Summer: Book first entry (8:30am)
- Little shade, very hot in summer
- Winter: Anytime fine

**Vatican Museums**
- Air conditioned (refuge from heat)
- Crowded year-round
- Friday nights in summer (less hot)

**Outdoor Dining**
- Perfect: April-June, September-October
- Summer: After 8pm only comfortable
- Winter: Indoor or heated terraces

## Extreme Weather

### Heat Waves
- Can exceed 40°C in summer
- Heat warnings issued
- Stay hydrated
- Avoid midday sun
- Free water from nasoni (fountains)

### Thunderstorms
- Summer afternoon storms possible
- Usually brief and intense
- Seek shelter
- Pass quickly

### Flooding
- Rare but possible with heavy rain
- Tiber has flood control
- Underground areas may flood

### Snow
- Very rare (once every few years)
- City shuts down when it happens
- Beautiful but chaotic

## Best Times Summary

### For Weather
1. **Late April - Early June**: Perfect
2. **September - Mid October**: Excellent
3. **Early-Mid April**: Good (variable)
4. **Late October - November**: Acceptable

### For Crowds
1. **January-February** (except holidays): Lowest
2. **November**: Low
3. **March (before Easter)**: Moderate
4. **December (mid-month)**: Moderate

### For Value
1. **January-February**: Best deals
2. **November**: Good deals
3. **July-August**: Some deals (off-peak for business)

### Overall Best
- **Late September - October**: Weather + crowds + value balanced
- **May**: Weather perfect, crowds manageable
- **April (non-Easter)**: Excellent all around

### Avoid If Possible
- **Easter Week**: Extremely crowded
- **August 15 (Ferragosto)**: Many closures
- **July-August** (unless heat-tolerant): Very hot
