# North SF Neighborhoods

## Marina District

**Best for:** Athletic types, young professionals, dating scene

**Character:** Upscale waterfront living. Running paths, Crissy Field, restaurants. "Bro" culture reputation.

| Rent | 1BR: $3,200-3,800 | 2BR: $4,500-6,000 |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|

**Pros:** Beautiful, safe, waterfront access, good restaurants
**Cons:** Far from downtown, limited nightlife, parking difficult

**Transit:** Bus lines (no BART nearby)
**Must-do:** Crissy Field walks, Palace of Fine Arts, Chestnut Street dining

---

## Pacific Heights

**Best for:** Wealthy residents, families, views

**Character:** SF's most expensive neighborhood. Mansions, incredible views, old money.

| Rent | 1BR: $4,000-5,000+ | 2BR: $6,000-8,000+ |
|------|---------------------|---------------------|

**Pros:** Ultra-safe, beautiful architecture, amazing views
**Cons:** Very expensive, car recommended, quiet

**Transit:** Bus lines, far from BART
**Must-do:** Lyon Street Steps, Fillmore Street shopping, mansion-spotting

---

## North Beach

**Best for:** History lovers, Italian food, nightlife

**Character:** "Little Italy" — historic Italian neighborhood. Cafés, bars, Beat Generation history.

| Rent | 1BR: $2,800-3,400 | 2BR: $3,800-5,000 |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|

**Pros:** Character, great food, walkable, central
**Cons:** Steep hills, tourist adjacent, parking impossible

**Transit:** Buses, cable car nearby
**Must-do:** City Lights Bookstore, Caffe Trieste, Tony's Pizza

---

## Cow Hollow

**Best for:** Young professionals, dining, shopping

**Character:** Between Marina and Pacific Heights. Union Street shops and restaurants.

| Rent | 1BR: $3,000-3,600 | 2BR: $4,200-5,500 |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|

**Pros:** Great dining, safe, walkable
**Cons:** Expensive, limited transit

**Transit:** Buses
**Must-do:** Union Street shopping, brunch spots

---

## Presidio

**Best for:** Nature lovers, families, quiet seekers

**Character:** Former military base, now national park. Forest trails, beaches, quiet.

| Rent | 1BR: $2,500-3,200 | 2BR: $3,500-4,500 |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|

**Pros:** Nature, safe, unique setting, near bridge
**Cons:** Far from everything, car needed

**Transit:** PresidiGo shuttle, buses
**Must-do:** Crissy Field, Presidio trails, Walt Disney Museum
