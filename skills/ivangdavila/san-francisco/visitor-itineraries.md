# San Francisco Itineraries

| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 9am | Cable car from Powell | California line shorter wait |
| 10am | Fisherman's Wharf | Sea lions at Pier 39 |

## Three Days (Essential)

### Day 1: Waterfront + Downtown
- Cable car experience
- Chinatown walking tour (Ross Alley)
- Ferry Building lunch
- Pier 39 sea lions
- North Beach dinner (Tony's Pizza)

### Day 2: Parks + Bridge
- Golden Gate Park (pick: Academy of Sciences OR de Young)
- Quick Haight-Ashbury walk
- Golden Gate Bridge (walk across)
- Sausalito (ferry back)
- Marina District dinner

### Day 3: Culture + Views
- Alcatraz (book ahead!)
- Mission District (murals, Dolores Park)
- Tacos at El Farolito
- Twin Peaks sunset
- Castro dinner

## Day Trips from SF

| Destination | Drive | Why | Tips |
|-------------|-------|-----|------|
| **Muir Woods** | 30 min | Giant redwoods | Book parking ahead |
| **Sausalito** | 15 min | Waterfront town | Ferry there, drive back |
| **Napa Valley** | 1 hr | Wine tasting | Book tastings ahead |
| **Sonoma** | 45 min | Wine, more relaxed | Better food than Napa |
| **Point Reyes** | 1 hr | Wildlife, beaches | Elephant seals! |
| **Monterey** | 2 hr | Aquarium, 17-Mile Drive | Full day needed |
| **Carmel** | 2.5 hr | Charming beach town | Combine with Monterey |
| **Half Moon Bay** | 45 min | Coastal escape | Mavericks surf spot |
| **Santa Cruz** | 1.5 hr | Beach boardwalk | Family-friendly |
| **Yosemite** | 3.5 hr | National park | Needs overnight |

## Best Times to Visit

| Month | Weather | Crowds | Events |
|-------|---------|--------|--------|
| **Sept-Oct** | Warmest, sunny | Medium | Fleet Week, Hardly Strictly Bluegrass |
| **Nov-Dec** | Cooler, holidays | Lower | Holiday markets |
| **Jan-Feb** | Rainy | Lowest | Chinese New Year parade |
| **Mar-May** | Mild, some rain | Medium | Cherry blossoms |
| **Jun-Aug** | FOG (cold!) | Highest | Pride (June), Outside Lands (Aug) |
