# Visitor Tips — San Francisco

## Weather Reality

| Myth | Reality |
|------|---------|
| "California = warm" | SF summers are COLD and foggy |
| "I don't need a jacket" | You always need layers |
| "It's sunny" | Microclimates vary by neighborhood |

### What to Pack
- Layers (always)
- Light jacket or fleece
- Comfortable walking shoes (hills!)
- No shorts-only outfits

### Temperature by Area
- **Mission/Castro:** Sunniest, warmest
- **Sunset/Richmond:** Foggy, cold
- **Downtown:** Wind tunnels

---

## Transport Basics

### Getting Around
- **Muni Metro/Bus** — $3/ride or $5 day pass
- **BART** — To airport, East Bay
- **Cable Cars** — $8/ride (tourist experience)
- **Uber/Lyft** — Best for hills and night

### Apps
- **Muni Mobile** — Pay for transit
- **Citymapper** — Navigation
- **Uber/Lyft** — Rideshare

### Tips
- Walk less than you think (hills!)
- Cable car California line has shorter waits
- BART closes at midnight
- Airport: BART to SFO is cheapest ($10)

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| No jacket | Always bring layers |
| Too much walking planned | Use Uber for hills |
| Not booking Alcatraz | Book first thing |
| Eating at Fisherman's Wharf | Just see sea lions, eat elsewhere |
| Staying near Tenderloin | Research hotel location carefully |
| Expecting warm summer | Sept-Oct is warmest |
| Driving everywhere | Parking is expensive and hard |

---

## Safety Quick Guide

- **Main risks:** Car break-ins, property theft, some areas
- **Car:** NEVER leave anything visible (even empty bags)
- **Phone:** Be aware, especially in transit
- **Avoid:** Tenderloin, Mid-Market, 6th-8th St SOMA

**Emergency:** 911 | Non-emergency: 311
