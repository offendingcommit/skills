# Central SF Neighborhoods

## Hayes Valley

**Best for:** Trendy urbanites, shoppers, central location

**Character:** Boutique shopping, hip restaurants, near Opera House. Central and walkable.

| Rent | 1BR: $3,200-4,000 | 2BR: $4,500-6,000 |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|

**Pros:** Central, trendy, great food/shopping, safe
**Cons:** Expensive, small area, near Tenderloin edge

**Transit:** MUNI Metro, buses (excellent)
**Must-do:** Patricia's Green, Smitten Ice Cream, boutique shopping

## Nob Hill

**Best for:** Classic SF experience, views, history

**Character:** Historic elegance, cable cars, grand hotels. Steep hills but beautiful.

| Rent | 1BR: $2,800-3,500 | 2BR: $4,000-5,500 |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|

**Pros:** Beautiful, central, historic, safe
**Cons:** Very hilly, parking impossible, expensive

**Transit:** Cable cars, buses
**Must-do:** Grace Cathedral, Top of the Mark views

---

## Russian Hill

**Best for:** Views, quiet central living

**Character:** Residential, famous for Lombard Street (crooked street). Great views, quiet.

| Rent | 1BR: $2,800-3,400 | 2BR: $4,000-5,000 |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|

**Pros:** Views, safe, walkable to North Beach
**Cons:** Very hilly, limited dining options

**Transit:** Cable cars, buses
**Must-do:** Lombard Street views (from below), Ina Coolbrith Park
