# Where to Stay — San Francisco

## Budget ($100-200/night)

| Area | Pros | Cons |
|------|------|------|
| **Marina District** | Safe, bridge views, motels | Far from downtown |
| **Sunset District** | Very safe, near park | Foggy, residential |
| **Richmond District** | Safe, great Asian food | Far from attractions |

**Budget options:**
- HI San Francisco Fisherman's Wharf — Hostel, great location
- Motel 6 Lombard — Basic but cheap
- Hotel Griffon — Affordable, waterfront
- Beck's Motor Lodge — Castro, retro charm

---

## Mid-Range ($200-350/night)

| Area | Pros | Cons |
|------|------|------|
| **Fisherman's Wharf** | Central, family-friendly | Noisy, touristy |
| **North Beach** | Italian charm, restaurants | Steep hills |
| **Nob Hill** | Elegant, cable car access | Uphill everywhere |
| **SOMA** | Modern, near museums | Mixed safety by block |

**Mid-range options:**
- Hotel Vitale — Embarcadero, waterfront
- Hotel Zephyr — Fisherman's Wharf, fun design
- Phoenix Hotel — Rock and roll history
- Hotel Triton — Union Square area, boutique

---

## Luxury ($350+/night)

| Area | Pros | Cons |
|------|------|------|
| **Pacific Heights** | Mansions, ultra-safe | Far from action |
| **Presidio** | Park setting, quiet | Need car/Uber |
| **Union Square** | Central, theaters | Near Tenderloin edge |
| **Nob Hill** | Historic grandeur | $$$$ |

**Luxury options:**
- Fairmont San Francisco — Iconic Nob Hill
- Ritz-Carlton — Classic luxury
- Palace Hotel — Historic downtown
- The Lodge at the Presidio — Park setting
- Cavallo Point — Marin side, bridge views

---

## By Situation

| Need | Best Area |
|------|-----------|
| **First visit, see everything** | Fisherman's Wharf, North Beach |
| **Business** | SOMA, Financial District |
| **Nightlife** | Mission, Marina |
| **Families** | Fisherman's Wharf, Marina |
| **Budget** | Richmond, Sunset |
| **Romance** | Pacific Heights, Presidio |
| **Tech visit** | SOMA (near offices) |

---

## Areas to AVOID for Hotels

| Area | Why |
|------|-----|
| **Tenderloin** | Crime, drugs, homeless — no matter how cheap |
| **Mid-Market / Civic Center** | Dangerous at night |
| **6th-8th St SOMA** | Drug activity |
| **Union Square south side** | Too close to Tenderloin |

**Rule:** If a hotel seems too cheap for SF, research the exact block carefully.
