# Attractions Guide — San Francisco

## Must-See (Worth Your Time)

| Attraction | Why | Tips |
|-----------|-----|------|
| **Alcatraz Island** | Immersive history, excellent audio tour | Book 2-4 weeks ahead, sells out |
| **Golden Gate Bridge** | Iconic, spectacular views | Walk/bike across. Views from Baker Beach, Fort Point, Crissy Field |
| **Golden Gate Park** | Larger than Central Park | Japanese Tea Garden, Academy of Sciences, de Young Museum |
| **Chinatown** | Oldest, largest in North America | Authentic food, explore Ross Alley |
| **Cable Cars** | UNESCO heritage, unique | California line = shorter wait |
| **Twin Peaks** | 360° city views | Go at sunset, bring jacket |
| **Pier 39 Sea Lions** | Free, entertaining | Best in morning |
| **Muir Woods** | Giant redwoods | 30 min drive, book parking ahead |
| **Mission Dolores Park** | Local atmosphere, skyline views | Bring picnic, young crowd |
| **Ferry Building** | Farmers market, artisan food | Saturday market best |

---

## Overrated (Skip or Quick Visit)

| Skip | Why | Instead |
|------|-----|---------|
| **Fisherman's Wharf** | Tourist trap, mediocre food | Sea lions only, eat elsewhere |
| **Union Square** | Mall stores, declining area | Mission or Hayes Valley shopping |
| **Lombard Street drive** | Marketing gimmick | View from below, don't wait in line |
| **Haight-Ashbury** | Past its moment | 30 min walk is enough |
| **Pier 39 beyond sea lions** | Generic, overpriced | Just see the sea lions |
| **Madame Tussauds** | Same as everywhere | Any museum instead |

---

## Maybe Attractions

| Attraction | Verdict | Notes |
|------------|---------|-------|
| **Exploratorium** | ✅ Great for kids/curious adults | Book ahead on weekends |
| **de Young Museum** | ✅ Worth it | Tower has free views |
| **Academy of Sciences** | ✅ Excellent | Planetarium, aquarium, rainforest |
| **Ghirardelli Square** | ⚠️ Quick visit | Free chocolate sample, then leave |
| **Coit Tower** | ⚠️ Nice views | Murals inside, not essential |
| **Painted Ladies** | ⚠️ Photo op | 5 minutes is enough |

## Free Attractions

| Attraction | What | Tips |
|------------|------|------|
| **Golden Gate Bridge** | Walk across | Start from Welcome Center |
| **Crissy Field** | Beach, bridge views | Great for picnics |
| **Mission murals** | Street art | Balmy Alley, Clarion Alley |
| **Lands End** | Coastal trail | Sutro Baths ruins |
| **Fort Point** | Historic fort, bridge views | Under the bridge |
| **Farmers markets** | Food, atmosphere | Ferry Building (Sat), Alemany |
| **City Lights Bookstore** | Beat generation history | North Beach |
