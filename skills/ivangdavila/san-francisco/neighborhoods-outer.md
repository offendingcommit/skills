# Outer SF Neighborhoods

## Outer Richmond

**Best for:** Families, Asian food lovers, budget-conscious

**Character:** Diverse, residential, excellent Asian restaurants. Near Golden Gate Park and Ocean Beach.

| Rent | 1BR: $2,200-2,800 | 2BR: $3,000-3,800 |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|

**Pros:** Affordable, safe, great food, near park
**Cons:** Foggy, far from downtown, quieter

**Transit:** Buses, far from BART
**Must-do:** Clement Street restaurants, dim sum, Land's End hike

---

## Outer Sunset

**Best for:** Surfers, beach lovers, budget-conscious

**Character:** Beach town vibe within SF. Laid-back, residential, excellent Asian food.

| Rent | 1BR: $2,100-2,600 | 2BR: $2,800-3,500 |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|

**Pros:** Most affordable, safe, beach access, chill vibe
**Cons:** Very foggy, far from everything, quiet

**Transit:** N-Judah MUNI, buses
**Must-do:** Ocean Beach sunset, Irving Street restaurants, Andytown Coffee

---

## Inner Richmond

**Best for:** Foodies, park lovers, balance of price/location

**Character:** Excellent restaurants, close to Golden Gate Park. Better transit than outer.

| Rent | 1BR: $2,400-3,000 | 2BR: $3,200-4,200 |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|

**Pros:** Great food, park access, safer than downtown
**Cons:** Foggy, limited nightlife

**Transit:** Buses, closer to downtown than outer
**Must-do:** Burma Superstar, Geary Street restaurants

---

## Inner Sunset

**Best for:** Students (near UCSF), park access, quieter living

**Character:** College town vibe, near Golden Gate Park and UCSF. Good food, quiet.

| Rent | 1BR: $2,300-2,900 | 2BR: $3,000-4,000 |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|

**Pros:** Near park, good food, safe, student vibe
**Cons:** Foggy, limited nightlife

**Transit:** N-Judah MUNI
**Must-do:** 9th & Irving restaurants, Golden Gate Park

---

## Cole Valley

**Best for:** Families, park lovers, village feel

**Character:** Small, charming neighborhood near Haight and Golden Gate Park. Village atmosphere.

| Rent | 1BR: $2,800-3,400 | 2BR: $3,800-5,000 |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|

**Pros:** Near park, safe, community feel, sunny (relative)
**Cons:** Small, limited options, quiet

**Transit:** N-Judah MUNI
**Must-do:** Cole Street shops, Tank Hill views
