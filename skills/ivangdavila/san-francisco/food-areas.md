# Food by Neighborhood — San Francisco

**Known for:** Tacos, burritos, brunch, trendy dinner

| Type | Spots |
|------|-------|
| **Taquerias** | El Farolito, La Taqueria, Taqueria Cancún |
| **Brunch** | Tartine, Foreign Cinema, Craftsman and Wolves |
| **Dinner** | Flour + Water (pasta), Californios ($$$$) |
| **Bars with food** | Trick Dog, ABV |
