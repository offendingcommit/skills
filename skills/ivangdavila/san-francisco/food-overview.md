# Food Overview — San Francisco

SF pioneered farm-to-table dining. Exceptional for Mission burritos, sourdough, seafood (cioppino, Dungeness crab), and world-class fine dining.

## Iconic SF Foods

| Food | What | Where |
|------|------|-------|
| **Mission Burrito** | Giant, rice inside, foil-wrapped | El Farolito, La Taqueria |
| **Sourdough Bread** | SF's signature tangy bread | Tartine (local), Boudin (tourist) |
| **Cioppino** | Tomato seafood stew, invented here | Tadich Grill, Sotto Mare |
| **Dungeness Crab** | Local crab, season Nov-June | Swan Oyster Depot |
| **Irish Coffee** | Invented at Buena Vista Café | Buena Vista (Wharf) |
| **Dim Sum** | Cantonese small plates | Richmond District |
| **It's-It** | Ice cream + oatmeal cookie | Any grocery |
